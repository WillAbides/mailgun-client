Repository - willroden.github.io

https://github.com/willroden/mailgun-client/tree/gh-pages/io/github/willroden/mailgun-client

Note that this repository isn’t browsable. You can use it in your pom.xml like this:

<repositories>
    <repository>
        <id>mailgun-client-mvn-repo</id>
        <url>https://raw.githubusercontent.com/willroden/mailgun-client/mvn-repo</url>
        <snapshots>
            <enabled>true</enabled>
            <updatePolicy>always</updatePolicy>
        </snapshots>
    </repository>
</repositories>
